Need Monero?

If you do not already have XMR, the usual process is:

  1. Install a Monero-compatible wallet. The official Monero site lists desktop, mobile, light, and hardware wallet options. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}
  2. Buy XMR through an exchange, a peer-to-peer market, mining, or by receiving it for goods or services. The official Monero site notes that many centralized and decentralized exchanges list XMR. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}
  3. Withdraw the XMR to your own wallet.
  4. Return to this page and send the donation from your wallet.

For wallet setup and payment basics, use the official Monero resources rather than random download links. Monero’s own documentation explains receiving via address or QR code and notes that funds require confirmations before they are safely spendable. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}
