Need Monero?

If you do not already have XMR, the general process is:

  1. Install a Monero-compatible wallet from the official Monero website or another trusted source.
  2. Buy XMR through an exchange, peer-to-peer marketplace, mining, or by receiving it from someone else.
  3. Withdraw the XMR to your own Monero wallet.
  4. Return to this page and send the donation from your wallet.

Availability of XMR varies by country and platform. Always verify that the service you use is legitimate, supports Monero withdrawals, and is legal in your jurisdiction.
