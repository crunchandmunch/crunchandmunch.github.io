How to Donate with Monero

This walkthrough is for visitors who want to donate with Monero but may not already have a Monero wallet or XMR. It explains the process in plain terms.

Monero is the only asset accepted at this donation address. Do not send Bitcoin, Ethereum, USDT, or any other cryptocurrency to the Monero address.

The short version

  1. Install a Monero wallet.
  2. Get some XMR.
  3. Send the XMR to the donation address.

If you already have a Monero wallet with XMR in it, you can skip directly to the donation step.

Step 1: Install a Monero wallet

A wallet is the app that lets you receive, hold, and send Monero. Use a Monero-compatible wallet from a trusted source.

A good starting point is the official Monero wallet download page: https://www.getmonero.org/downloads/

After installing the wallet, follow its setup instructions. The wallet may give you a recovery phrase, seed, or keys. Keep those private. Anyone who gets access to your recovery information may be able to access your funds.

Step 2: Get XMR

XMR is the currency used by Monero. To donate with Monero, you need some XMR in your own Monero wallet.

The usual path is to buy XMR from a service that supports Monero and then withdraw the XMR to your own wallet. Make sure the service allows actual XMR withdrawals. Some platforms only let you trade exposure to the price and do not let you withdraw real Monero.

Availability depends on your country and the platform you use. Only use services that are legal in your jurisdiction.

Step 3: Withdraw XMR to your own wallet

If you bought XMR on an exchange or another service, withdraw it to the receiving address shown inside your own Monero wallet.

This step matters because the donation should be sent from a wallet that you control. After the withdrawal completes and your wallet shows the XMR balance, you are ready to donate.

Step 4: Send the donation

Return to the donation page and either scan the QR code or copy the Monero address.

Open Wallet App

If scanning the QR code

  1. Open your Monero wallet app.
  2. Choose the send option.
  3. Scan the QR code on the donation page.
  4. Enter the amount of XMR you want to donate.
  5. Review the address and amount carefully.
  6. Send the transaction.

If copying the address

  1. Copy the donation address.
  2. Open your Monero wallet.
  3. Choose the send option.
  4. Paste the address into the recipient field.
  5. Enter the amount of XMR you want to donate.
  6. Review the address and amount carefully.
  7. Send the transaction.

Step 5: Wait for confirmation

After sending, the Monero network needs time to confirm the transaction. Your wallet may show the transaction as pending before it is fully confirmed. That is normal.

Safety checks

  • Only send XMR to this address.
  • Do not send other cryptocurrencies to a Monero address.
  • Make sure the copied address matches the address shown on the donation page.
  • Do not share your wallet seed, recovery phrase, private spend key, or private view key.
  • Use only wallet software and services you trust.
If you are unsure, send a small test amount first. After confirming that it worked, you can send the rest.
